Bluetooth Bike Shifter

The Problem

Mechanical shifting systems are only compatible with one gear configuration (1x12 for example)

Shift distance between specific gears cannot be adjusted with a mechanical system

Mechanical shifters are not aesthetically pleasing; there is a large cable crossing over in front of the bike

Existing wireless shifters are VERY expensive ($1500+) and are vulnerable to impacts
